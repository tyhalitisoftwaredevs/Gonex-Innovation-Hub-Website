How to help

Ways to support Gonex

Money matters, but it's not the only way. Time, skills, and a simple share can change someone's life.

Fund

A direct financial contribution funds equipment, internet, program delivery, and staff. Every rand goes to work immediately. Contact us to discuss donation options, one-off or recurring.

Sponsor

Sponsor a program cohort, a piece of equipment, or a month of operations. Corporate sponsors receive recognition, reporting, and an authentic CSI story rooted in real community impact.

Volunteer & Mentor

Share your skills. Volunteer as a facilitator, mentor a founder, or run a workshop. Even a few hours a month can shift someone's trajectory in a meaningful way.

Spread the word

Follow us on social media, share our story, and tell people about Gonex. Visibility is everything for a new hub — your network reaches people we haven't met yet.
