Programs & Services

Practical programs designed to help you learn, build, and grow.

Six structured programs and flexible workspace access — open to anyone in the region ready to take the next step.

What we offer

Our programs

Digital Literacy Training

Youth & adults 6–12 weeks Free / subsidised

From basic computer use to internet navigation, productivity tools, and entry-level coding — this program meets you where you are and builds a foundation for everything digital. No prior experience required.

  • Computer basics, file management, and internet use
  • Google Workspace / Microsoft Office productivity tools
  • Introduction to coding and web design
  • Online freelancing and remote work basics

Entrepreneurship Development

Aspiring founders 10–16 weeks Free / subsidised

A structured incubation program taking you from idea to validated business concept. Covers business fundamentals, pitch development, funding pathways, and launch planning — with one-on-one mentorship throughout.

  • Idea validation and market research
  • Business model development and financial basics
  • Pitch deck creation and investor-readiness
  • Access to funding networks and grant information
Express interest

Co-working Space Access

Freelancers & professionals Daily / Monthly Membership fee applies

A professional, quiet environment with high-speed internet, workstations, printing, and meeting room access. Perfect for remote workers, job seekers, students, and anyone who needs a productive space to get things done.

  • High-speed fibre internet access
  • Dedicated workstations and hot desks
  • Printing, scanning, and photocopying
  • Bookable meeting rooms for teams and interviews

Innovation Programs & Events

Open to all Weekend / intensive Free to enter

Hackathons, design challenges, community innovation labs, and collaborative build sessions. These short-format events bring people together to solve real local problems with practical ideas — and are open to everyone.

  • Themed hackathons with prizes and mentorship
  • Community design-thinking workshops
  • Innovation lab: prototype and test ideas fast
  • Networking events connecting makers and founders

Business Support Services

Early-stage businesses Ongoing Membership benefit

Practical, hands-on support for businesses that are already operating or just starting. From CIPC registration to marketing strategy and financial compliance — we help you do the things that keep a business running.

  • Business registration and compliance guidance (CIPC, SARS)
  • Basic bookkeeping and financial literacy
  • Marketing: social media, branding, and customer acquisition
  • Access to mentors and industry advisors

Skills Development

Job seekers & youth 4–8 weeks Free / subsidised

Targeted short courses in high-demand skills: graphic design, social media management, data entry, customer service, and more. Designed to get you employment-ready or freelance-ready as quickly as possible.

  • Graphic design fundamentals (Canva, basic Adobe)
  • Social media management for businesses
  • Data entry and administrative skills
  • Communication, CV writing, and interview prep
