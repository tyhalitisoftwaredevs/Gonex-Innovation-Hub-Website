About Gonex Innovation Hub

Pioneering technology and innovation in rural Eastern Cape

Our Vision

To transform underserved communities, especially black youth and women, through scalable technology-based solutions that will create sustainable economic development.

Our Mission

To accelerate digital and economic inclusion through technology and innovation, operating as a 100% black-owned Level 1 B-BBEE organization focused on high-impact development programs.

Strategic Location

Located in Sterkspruit, Eastern Cape, near the Lesotho border, we will provide unique cross-border opportunities and serve as the first rural technology center in the region.

The Challenge We're Addressing

  • 60%+ youth unemployment in our community
  • Zero access to digital skills training within 100km radius
  • R290 billion digital economy growing, but rural youth excluded
  • 2.3 million youth in Eastern Cape with minimal digital skills access

Our Solution

Gonex Innovation Hub will serve as the first rural technology center in the region, delivering combined innovation, accessibility, and efficiency to create long-term impact through comprehensive digital skills training and entrepreneurship development.

Our Programs & Services

Comprehensive support for digital transformation and entrepreneurship

Digital Literacy Training

We will offer Microsoft Office, internet skills, email communication, and comprehensive coding bootcamps designed for beginners to advanced learners.

  • Basic computer skills
  • Web development
  • Programming fundamentals
  • Digital communication

Entrepreneurship Development

We plan to provide business planning, financial literacy, startup support, and comprehensive mentorship programs for aspiring entrepreneurs.

  • Business plan development
  • Financial literacy training
  • Startup incubation

Co-working Space

  • High-speed internet access

Business Support Services

  • Business development

Skills Development
