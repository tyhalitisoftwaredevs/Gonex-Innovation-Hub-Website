Our mission

Why Gonex exists

The Eastern Cape has talent, ambition, and ideas. It lacks the infrastructure to turn them into action. We're building that infrastructure — one program, one skill, one person at a time.

Learn

Practical digital and entrepreneurial skills training. No prior experience needed — just the drive to grow.

Build

A physical space with internet, computers, tools, and mentorship to move from idea to prototype to reality.

Grow

Business support, community networks, and ongoing programs to help you scale and sustain what you start.

Where we're headed

The bigger picture

"We're not waiting for someone else to fix the opportunity gap in the Eastern Cape. We're building the infrastructure ourselves."

Gonex is in its founding chapter. We are honest about where we are: a new hub with a clear mission, a committed team, and a growing community. The goals below are where we are headed — not where we've already been. Every person who joins, supports, or spreads the word gets us there faster.

1

Launch & stabilise the hub

Open the space, enrol the first cohort of members, and establish our core program delivery.

2

Expand program reach

Partner with schools, NGOs, and local government to extend digital training to surrounding communities.

3

Build the alumni network

Connect graduates with jobs, funding, and each other — creating a self-sustaining ecosystem of opportunity.
